Imagining a world where you can synchronize with your pets

About

PawsIO is an integrated pet exercise and health monitoring app which harnesses the power of the Internet of Things to track each of your pets exercise session.

Insights

With PawsIO, you now have the ability to closely monitor your pets daily activity in a clear and concise manner. Use it to gauge if there needs (and yours!) have been met.

Advanced Data

PawsIO collects data related to the distance your pet travels, the ambient temperature around them, and noise intensity. Combined with insight from your veterenarian, you can quickly develop a solid exercise routine for you and your pet!
